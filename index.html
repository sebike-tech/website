COMMUNITY HERBAL EDUCATION PROGRAM

Rationale

Based on the findings of the study, results revealed that Lumad adults in Glan,
Sarangani Province have high knowledge and frequent use of medicinal herbs, however,
moderate perceived usefulness and duration of use indicate a gap between knowledge and sustained,
confident application. These concerns affect the safe, confident, and effective use of medicinal herbs for health management.
To address this, a Community Herbal Education Program is proposed as an intervention designed
to strengthen proper herbal medication practices through culturally responsive education,
integration of indigenous and scientific knowledge, and community-based training.
This program aims to promote responsible use of medicinal herbs and improve health outcomes among Lumad adults.
